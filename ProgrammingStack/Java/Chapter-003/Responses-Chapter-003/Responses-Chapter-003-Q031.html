Floating point literals can be represented in 2 ways:-

1. Standard Notation:- Literal is represented by whole number component, followed by a decimal point, followed by the fractional part. Example,
9.445 and 8433.73 are examples of standard representation of floating point literals.

2. Scientific Notation:- Literal is represented as standard notation followed by suffix E and power of 10. Example,
6.033E45 and 413.55E-89 are examples of scientific representation of floating point literals.
